General Usage

Miku Chan is designed as a collective music tool for the Indian Vocaloid scene. By interacting with the bot, you acknowledge that:

  • You will not use the bot for commercial broadcasting.
  • The bot is provided "as is" without warranty of uptime.
  • Command spamming is prohibited and results in a node-ban.

Privacy Statement:

We do not record audio. We only track server IDs to maintain voice connection stability across our regional clusters.

Content Rights

Miku Chan streams music from public repositories and community-submitted archives. We respect the "P" (Producer) culture:

  • Metadata including Producer names will always be displayed.
  • We honor "Do Not Redistribute" requests from artists.
  • The bot does not store permanent copies of copyrighted tracks.

Termination

The Vocaloid.in collective reserves the right to restrict access to Miku Chan if a server is found to be hosting malicious content or violating Discord's primary Safety Guidelines.
